# Origin

Publishing operations are not just a schedule problem. They are an editorial standards, approval, and evidence problem. The moment a claim changes or a disclosure drifts, teams need a surface that maps publish pressure to downstream review queues, proof packets, and the next safe move.

We built `editorial-standards-approval-desk` to make that operating layer explicit. The point is not to replace a CMS or brand system. The point is to show what a Media / Publishing operator surface should look like when the audience needs to manage copy pressure, downstream approvals, and publishing posture with real revenue consequences attached.
